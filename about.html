Who are you?
You may ask.

I'm Taishiro Mizutani, and all my friends call me Tai. I'm a college student born in Japan but raised in both Japan and China. My big journey began in 2007 when I moved to Chengdu, China with my parents where I dived into an international school without prior knowledge of English.

This experience enabled me to acquire three different languages at a young age, leading to many more opportunities such as study-abroad to a boarding school in the United States which sparked my interest in Business.

I am living my best life, not because of my effort, but because of the blessed environment I am in, with great friends that motivate me and a loving family that believes and supports my decision. Thus, my goal is to make the most of the environment I am in and give it back to the community by making the world a better place.

When I am not working, you will usually see me chilling with my friends, reading a book at a cafe, going all out at a party, or skiing on beautiful mountains during winter season.

If you have anything else you want to know about, please feel comfortable to send me an email, take a sneak peek at my blog, or connect with me through facebook or linkedin.
